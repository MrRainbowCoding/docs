1. Navigate to the Home page. 
   <Frame>
     ![1 1](/images/create-account/1-1.jpg)
   </Frame>

2. Click "Sign In" 
   <Frame>
     ![2](/images/create-account/2.jpg)
   </Frame>
3. Type your email in 
   <Frame>
     ![3 1](/images/create-account/3-1.jpg)
   </Frame>
4. Type in a password. Ensure it is strong, we won't check! 
   <Frame>
     ![4](/images/create-account/4.jpg)
   </Frame>
5. Click "Create account" 
   <Frame>
     ![5 1](/images/create-account/5-1.jpg)
   </Frame>
6. Finished!! This is an example of what your page should look like if you choose a name afterwards. 
   <Frame>
     ![6 1](/images/create-account/6-1.jpg)
   </Frame>
