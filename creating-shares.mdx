title: "Creating Shares"
---

1. Navigate to the home page. 

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/creating-shares/1.jpeg"
    alt="1"
    title="1"
    className="mr-auto"
  />
</Frame>

2. Title your share whatever you'd like. 
   <Frame>
     ![2](/images/creating-shares/2.jpg)
   </Frame>
3. Type a description for your share. It can be whatever you want, or nothing! 
   <Frame>
     ![3](/images/creating-shares/3.jpg)
   </Frame>
4. Click an expiration time. If you want it to last forever, you'll need an account. See the guide on [Creating an Account](/create-account). 
   <Frame>
     ![4](/images/creating-shares/4.jpg)
   </Frame>
5. Click "Create Share". 
   <Frame>
     ![5](/images/5.jpg)
   </Frame>
6. Copy the link and share! Add files with the "Drag & drop files" button!
   <Frame>
     ![Ascreenshot (6)](/images/ascreenshot-\(6\).jpeg)
   </Frame>
